Client Perspective

A complete guide to using the EGCN platform as a business client.

1. How to Register

To start using EGCN, you need to create a new business account. Navigate to the login page and click "Don't have an account?". Fill in your company name, name, email, and password.

Create Account

Sign Up

2. How to Verify Email

After registering, you will be redirected to the OTP Verification screen. Check your inbox for a 6-digit code and enter it to activate your account.

Verify Your Email

3. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your central hub. It displays your company's health score, revenue metrics, ROAS, and recent activities. The sidebar provides navigation to all modules.

Dashboard

4. Daily Input

To keep your stats accurate, enter your daily revenue and ad spend. Navigate to "Daily Input", fill the form, and click "Log Daily Data".

Log Daily Data

5. Upgrading Your Plan

Unlock advanced features by upgrading your plan. Go to "Settings" > "Subscription", select a plan, and complete the secure Razorpay checkout.

6. How to Use Workspace

The Workspace is where you collaborate with your assigned Expert. You can upload files, chat in real-time, and join secure video calls.
